We choose people over projects.

Meridian Construction is a 100% employee-owned commercial interior general contractor. We've spent two decades proving that the best way to build better buildings is to build a better company first.

Our Story

We grew the way we build — deliberately, locally, and by earning the next relationship.

2001

Founded in Denver

Meridian starts as a small commercial interiors specialist serving the Front Range, generating roughly $12M in its early years.

2011

100% employee-owned

We convert to an ESOP, giving every employee a real stake in the company's future — and changing how we show up for clients.

2015

Southwest expansion

New offices open in Phoenix and Austin as technology and life-science clients pull us into fast-growing markets.

2019

Pacific Northwest

A Portland office anchors our Pacific NW practice, deepening our healthcare and hospitality expertise.

2022

Mountain West infill

Salt Lake City, Boise, and Nashville complete a seven-office footprint built around where our clients grow.

2026

$640M and 640+ owners

Today Meridian is one of the region's largest interiors-focused builders — still employee-owned, still people-first.

Employee Ownership

In 2011 we became 100% employee-owned through an ESOP. Every eligible employee receives company-funded shares that grow into a supplemental retirement benefit — at no cost to them. It's the reason accountability here isn't a policy; it's a mindset.
